1. Executive Summary

## Visuals
- KPI cards: Listings, Sales, Deleted Listings, Revenue, Search-to-Sale Ratio
- Line chart: Listings and sales over time
- Bar chart: Sales by category
- Scatter/bar chart: Searches vs purchases
- Bar chart: Average days to sale by category

Date, item category, user type, listing status.
